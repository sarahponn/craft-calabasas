006 / FAQ

COMMON QUESTIONS

How much does coworking cost at Craft?

We offer flexible membership tiers from hot desk access to private offices. Every tier includes gym access, 24/7 entry, and all amenities. Text or call for current pricing.

Is there really a gym included?

Yes ‚Äî every membership includes full access to Craft Boxing Club, our 6,000 sq ft gym on the first floor. Boxing classes, full weight room, cardio, infrared sauna, and personal training available.

Can I use the recording studios?

Studio access is separate from coworking memberships, but members get priority booking and discounted rates on our recording studios, podcast suites, and streaming rooms.

Is there parking?

Unlimited free parking right outside. We're also directly off the 101 freeway, making it easy to get here from anywhere in LA or Ventura County.

Can I bring guests or clients?

Yes. Members can bring guests to the lounge and meeting areas. We also have meeting rooms and a theater that can be reserved for client presentations.
